About Chhatija's

Chhatija's brings the fun and flavour of Indian street food into everyday snacking. Inspired by local favourites like pani puri, masala bhel, chowmein and more, every pack is crafted to deliver a burst of taste in every bite.

Whether it is school tiffin time, family get-togethers or late-night movie marathons, Chhatija's snacks are made to share, savour and smile over.
